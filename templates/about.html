Direct cloud access through HTTPs

In this method, Cisco products send usage information directly over the internet to CSSM on http://www.cisco.com; no additional components are needed for deployment.

Direct cloud access through an HTTPs Proxy

In direct cloud access through an HTTPs proxy deployment method, Cisco products send usage information over the internet through a proxy server—either a Smart Call Home Transport Gateway or off-the-shelf Proxy (such as Apache) to CSSM on http://www.cisco.com.

Mediated access through an on-premises

In mediated access through an on-premises collector deployment method, Cisco products send usage information to a locally connected collector, which acts as a local license authority. Periodically, the information is exchanged to keep the databases in synchronization.

Specific License Reservartion

SLR is a feature that allows using specific entitlements on the device and doesn’t require to be connected to CSSM.
